Burger

  • Time: 30
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Beef cutlet 1
Slice of cheese 1
Bread 2
Tomato 1
Salad 1

Step 1: Heat a pan and cook the beef cutlet until it is well done on both sides.
Step 2: Place the slice of cheese on the hot beef cutlet and wait until it melts.
Step 3: Wash the tomato and cut it into round slices.
Step 4: Wash the salad leaves and let them dry.
Step 5: Lightly toast the two slices of bread in a pan or toaster.
Step 6: Put one slice of bread on a plate and add the salad on top.
Step 7: Place the beef cutlet with melted cheese on the salad.
Step 8: Add tomato slices on top of the cheese.
Step 9: Cover with the second slice of bread.
Step 10: Your burger is ready to eat!

Scrambled Eggs

  • Time: 15
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Egg 2
Salt 1
Oil 1

Step 1: Break the eggs into a bowl and add salt.
Step 2: Heat oil in a pan.
Step 3: Pour the eggs into the pan and mix until cooked.
